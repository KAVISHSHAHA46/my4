ABOUT WHITEHAT JR:


WhiteHat Jr aims to empower children so that they view themselves as creators. The app helps children learn computer programming and encourages them to create games, animations, applications and more. ... The subjects range from data structure, app and game development to machine learning and space technology.



Karan Bajaj At the 11th edition of TechSparks, Karan Bajaj, Founder and CEO of WhiteHat Jr., spoke on enabling children to become architects of technology and the pool of women talent behind the edtech startup's successful growth and expansion.

Whitehat Jr has an online teacher interface that has two types of logins, one for recruited teachers and one for students who have paid for a programming course there. Through this web interface and video chat facility, a teacher and a student interact to learn a programming language i.e. JavaScript.



The company also acquired WhiteHat Jr in a deal worth $300 million. Almost after a month of announcing the acquisition, Byju's has pumped in Rs 136 crore. This is the maiden infusion by Byju's in the coding platform meant for school-going children.
